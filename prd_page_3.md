6. User Roles & Permissions
6.1 Protocol (VexiumVerse)
Issues server licenses
Collects protocol fees
Provides infrastructure
Cannot set local prices
6.2 Server Owner (National Government)
Capabilities:  - Create and register a world - Set tax bands (within protocol limits) - Appoint or enable
elections for mayors - Control national treasury spending
6.3 Mayor (Regional Government)
Capabilities:  -  Control  land  distribution  -  Set  permit  and  license  pricing  -  Manage  regional  resource
extraction - Compete for population and industry
Poor governance leads to migration and capital flight.
6.4 Business Owner
Capabilities:  - Operate production or service businesses - Manage payroll, inventory, and P&L - Participate
in production chains
Businesses cannot consume their own outputs.
6.5 Citizen
Capabilities:  - Maintain survival needs - Earn income via labor or ownership - Spend currency on goods and
services
7. Survival & Pressure Mechanics
Each citizen has three decaying needs: - Food - Water - Sleep
Effects of Neglect:  - Reduced productivity - Inability to operate businesses - Restricted movement or work
These mechanics ensure mandatory spending and continuous demand.•
